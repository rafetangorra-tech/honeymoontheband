# Pete Averso — Bass

Pete Averso is a Long Island bassist with a **decade-plus career** across live music and professional theatre. He's held the bass chair for productions at **the Argyle Theatre** and the **John W. Engeman Theater**, and performed at New York City rooms including **54 Below, The Bitter End, and The Village Underground**.

That theatrical background shows up live: tight charts, clean transitions, and a feel that locks the band in regardless of tempo or genre.

**Notable credits:** Argyle Theatre · John W. Engeman Theater · 54 Below · The Bitter End · The Village Underground
