# Alexandra Mendes — Lead Vocals

Alexandra Mendes is a New York–based vocalist with **two-plus decades of professional experience** in live music. Her dynamic range and commanding stage presence have anchored shows for some of the East Coast's most respected live-event companies, including **Hank Lane Music** and **Element Music**.

At Honeymoon she fronts everything from intimate ceremony moments to peak-energy reception sets — moving cleanly between Motown, modern pop, classic rock, and ballads without missing the room.

**Notable credits:** Hank Lane Music · Element Music · 20+ years of weddings, galas, and private events across the NY Tristate Area
