# Marc Lambert — Guitar

Marc Lambert is a New York guitarist with a **26-year career** spanning live performance, recording, and touring. He holds a **Bachelor's degree in Jazz Performance from the Conservatory of Music at SUNY Purchase College**, and has performed and recorded as a member of progressive groups **Painted In Exile** and **Drop The 4**.

On the road he's shared stages and tours with **Plini, Intervals, Scar Symmetry, and Arsis**, bringing a level of technical command that translates directly into the precision and pocket Honeymoon's live show is built on.

**Notable credits:** SUNY Purchase Conservatory of Music (B.M. Jazz Performance) · Painted In Exile · Drop The 4 · Toured with Plini, Intervals, Scar Symmetry, Arsis
