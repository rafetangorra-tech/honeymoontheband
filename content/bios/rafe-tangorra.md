# Rafe Tangorra — Drums

Rafe Tangorra is a New York drummer and musical director whose **25-year career** spans national touring, major-label recording, and the live-event circuit. He has toured nationally and **opened for Shawn Mendes, Miley Cyrus, Meghan Trainor, Fergie, Camila Cabello, the Backstreet Boys, and Flo Rida**, and was part of an **EPIC Records**–signed project behind the single **"Boo Hoo"** — co-founded with his brother and current Honeymoon vocalist, **Nick Tangorra**.

Off the road, Rafe co-founded **Rock Out Autism**, a national 501(c)(3) supporting people on the autism spectrum.
