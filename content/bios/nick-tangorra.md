# Nick Tangorra — Lead Vocals

Nick Tangorra is a Long Island pop singer and songwriter whose voice has clocked tens of millions of streams. His 2016 single **"Boo Hoo"** crossed **28 million streams**, his 2015 EP *One Step Closer* debuted at **#16 on the iTunes Pop chart**, and his 2012 debut *Teenage Love and Other Stories* hit **#20 on the pop charts**.

Before joining Honeymoon, Nick fronted the **Nick Tangorra Band**, the Long Island project he founded in 2012 with his brother — Honeymoon drummer Rafe Tangorra. Together the brothers also co-founded **Rock Out Autism**, a national 501(c)(3) supporting people on the autism spectrum.

Citing **Amy Winehouse, Bruno Mars, Gwen Stefani, and Justin Timberlake** as influences, Nick brings a recording-artist's polish and a frontman's command of the room to every Honeymoon show.
