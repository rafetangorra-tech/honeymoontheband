## What is Honeymoon?

Honeymoon is a six-piece live band based in the New York Tristate Area. We perform at weddings, private events, and corporate parties across NYC, Long Island, and Westchester.

## Who's in the band?

Honeymoon's lineup is Nick Tangorra (lead vocals), Alexandra Mendes (lead vocals), Marc Lambert (guitar), Frank Buffolino (keys), Pete Averso (bass), and Rafe Tangorra (drums). [Read the full bios.](/band)

## Where do you perform?

We're based in the New York Tristate Area and regularly play across **New York City** (all five boroughs), **Long Island** (Nassau and Suffolk Counties), and **Westchester County**. Travel to Connecticut, New Jersey, and beyond is available on request.

## What configurations do you offer?

Three:

- **Standard 6-piece** — our core lineup.
- **Extended with horns** — 6-piece plus added horn players for a fuller, brassier sound.
- **Band + DJ hybrid** — live band integrated with a DJ for seamless transitions and extended dance sets.

## How much does Honeymoon cost?

Pricing depends on configuration, date, location, and event scope. We send a custom quote for every inquiry. Email **honeymoonbandbookings@gmail.com** with your date, venue, and configuration and we'll get back to you with availability and pricing.

## How far in advance should I book?

Wedding-season dates (May–October) book up early — **6 to 12 months in advance is typical**, and prime Saturdays often go faster. For off-peak dates and weekday events we can sometimes accommodate shorter timelines. Reach out as soon as you have a date in mind. `<<CONFIRM>>`

## Can you play our ceremony, cocktail hour, and reception?

Yes. We're set up to cover the full event arc — ceremony, cocktail hour, reception, and late-night sets. You can also book us for the reception only.

## Do you take song requests?

Yes. Send your must-play list with your booking and we'll work it into the show. We're also happy to learn new songs for your event when there's time — just ask. `<<CONFIRM — confirm policy on number of new-song learns per event>>`

## Do you provide sound and lighting?

We bring full professional PA appropriate for the event size. Lighting is venue-dependent — we'll coordinate with your venue or recommend a lighting partner if needed. `<<CONFIRM — confirm whether lighting is included, optional, or always third-party>>`

## What kind of music do you play?

Modern pop, Motown, soul, funk, disco, classic rock, 2000s throwbacks, country, Latin, and ballads for ceremony and first-dance moments. [Browse the full song list.](/song-list)

## How long are your sets?

We tailor set length and break structure to your event timeline. A typical reception runs four hours of live music, structured around the night's flow (entrances, dinner, dances, open dancing). Longer events are easy to accommodate. `<<CONFIRM — confirm standard set length and break structure>>`

## Can we hear or see you live before booking?

Most of our current live media lives on Instagram — follow [**@honeymoon_theband**](https://www.instagram.com/honeymoon_theband) for recent reels and clips.

## How do I book?

Email **[honeymoonbandbookings@gmail.com](mailto:honeymoonbandbookings@gmail.com?subject=Honeymoon%20Booking%20Inquiry)** with your event date, venue, configuration, and any details you'd like to share. We'll reply with availability and pricing.
