## Configurations

### Standard 6-piece
Our core lineup: two lead vocalists, guitar, keys, bass, drums. The format we're built around — full sound, deep song list, designed to anchor a wedding or event from cocktail hour through the last dance.

### Extended (with horns)
Our 6-piece lineup plus added horn players. A fuller, brassier sound for clients who want more weight on Motown, funk, disco, and big-band-energy moments.

### Band + DJ hybrid
Live band performance integrated with a DJ. Live energy where you want it (key dances, peak sets, big moments) plus seamless DJ transitions and extended dance music. Best of both — no awkward gaps, no break in momentum.
