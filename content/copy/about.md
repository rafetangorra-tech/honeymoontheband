# About Honeymoon

Honeymoon is a six-piece live band built for weddings, private events, and corporate parties across the **New York Tristate Area**. Two lead vocalists, a tight rhythm section, and a deep song list — designed to keep your dance floor full from the first note to the last.

---

## What we do

We play the music your guests already love — modern pop, Motown, classic rock, disco, soul, and the ballads that make ceremony and first-dance moments land. Our standard show is a high-energy live performance built around your event's flow: cocktail hour, dinner, dancing, late-night.

We're full-service for the music side of your event. Bring us in for the reception only, or for the whole arc — ceremony, cocktail, reception.

---

## Where we play

Honeymoon is based in the New York Tristate Area and regularly performs across:

- **New York City** — all five boroughs
- **Long Island** — Nassau and Suffolk Counties
- **Westchester County**
- **Connecticut and New Jersey** — on request

Travel beyond the Tristate is available — [reach out](/booking) with your venue and date.

---

## How we're built

Six musicians, decades of combined stage experience. Two lead vocalists handle every era and energy level. The rhythm section comes from touring, theatre, and recording backgrounds — which means tight charts, clean transitions, and the kind of consistency that only shows up after thousands of nights on stage.

## Configurations

We offer three formats so the band fits the room:

- **Standard 6-piece** — our core lineup.
- **Extended with horns** — add horn players for a fuller, brassier sound.
- **Band + DJ hybrid** — live band paired with a DJ for seamless transitions and extended dance sets.
